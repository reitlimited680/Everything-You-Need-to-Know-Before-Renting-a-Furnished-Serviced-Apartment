## Understand the Rental Agreement

Before signing a lease, carefully review all rental conditions.

Confirm the following:
- Monthly rent
- Security deposit
- Payment schedule
- Lease duration
- Cancellation policy
- Maintenance responsibilities

Also, ask whether utility bills such as electricity, gas, water, internet and cleaning services are included in the rental fee.

Understanding these details beforehand helps avoid unexpected expenses and ensures complete transparency between you and the property owner.

## Safety Should Never Be Overlooked

- CCTV surveillance

These features provide greater peace of mind, especially if you're staying with family or planning a long-term rental.

One of the reasons Bashundhara R/A continues to attract residents is its reputation for being one of Dhaka's safest and most organized communities.

## Consider Overall Value, Not Just the Price

While the monthly rent for a Fully Furnished Services Apartment may appear higher than that of a traditional apartment, it's important to consider everything that's included.

When furniture, kitchen equipment, internet, utility setup, maintenance and housekeeping are factored into the total cost, serviced apartments often provide better overall value.

They also eliminate the time, effort and additional expenses involved in furnishing an empty apartment, making them an economical solution for many renters.

## Inspect Before You Commit

Whenever possible, visit the property before confirming you're booking. Check the following:
- Cleanliness
- Furniture quality
- Internet speed
- Water supply
- Kitchen equipment
- Overall maintenance

If you're renting from another city or country, ask for:
- Recent photos
- Virtual tour
- Customer reviews

Reputable serviced apartment providers are usually transparent and happy to answer your questions.

Taking these simple precautions can help ensure that the apartment meets your expectations.
